  %%-- Draw the mission info

        $\Box$ $\Box$ $\Box$ & Your warlord challenged the enemy warlord at
                               least once.\\[3pt]
        $\Box$ $\Box$ $\Box$ & Your warlord or an attached unit
                               eliminated the enemy warlord.\\[3pt]
        $\Box$ $\Box$ $\Box$ & Your warlord eliminated an opponent in at least
                               one challenge.\\[3pt]
        $\Box$ $\Box$ $\Box$ & Your warlord or an attached unit won
                               at least one close combat.\\[3pt]
        $\Box$ $\Box$ $\Box$ & Your warlord or an attached unit eliminated at
                               least one enemy unit through shooting.\\[3pt]
        $\Box$ $\Box$ $\Box$ & Your warlord or an attached unit scored an
                               objective marker at least once this game.\\[3pt]
        $\Box$ $\Box$ $\Box$ & Your warlord is wholly within the enemy
                               deployment zone.\\[3pt]
        $\Box$ $\Box$ $\Box$ & Your warlord is at least partially within~6''

    };
